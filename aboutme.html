About Me

Welcome to my Digital Space

My journey into technology began in childhood, fueled by a passion for gaming and problem-solving. This early fascination led me to explore the mechanics behind digital experiences, setting the foundation for a career in tech. Over the years, I built expertise in customer support and technical troubleshooting, working closely with users to bridge the gap between people and technology. As my understanding of digital ecosystems deepened, I transitioned into web development, specializing in front-end technologies and Solidity to craft interactive and decentralized solutions.

Blockchain and Web3 became a natural evolution of my work, allowing me to contribute to innovative projects that redefine financial inclusion and digital ownership. My background in psychology has played a key role in shaping my approach to user experience, system optimization, and quality assurance.

My Expertise

  • Web Development: Front-end development and Solidity programming for dApps and interactive platforms.
  • Blockchain & Web3: Passionate about Ethereum, EVM-compatible networks, and smart contract development.
  • Quality Assurance (QA): Manual testing, Agile methodologies, and ensuring seamless digital experiences.
  • Project Management: Skilled in Scrum, Jira, and Agile workflows to lead and optimize tech projects.
  • Tech Support & Customer Experience: Over a decade of experience in B2B and B2C technical support.
  • Game Development & Level Design: Crafting engaging gaming experiences, from mechanics to storytelling.

My Projects

I have worked on multiple blockchain and web projects, including Cuchulink, a circular savings platform leveraging smart contracts for financial empowerment. I actively participate in hackathons and buildathons, collaborating on innovative solutions.

In 2025, I took part in the Global Game Jam, co-developing Bubble Soul, a narrative-driven graphic novel game. Additionally, I contributed to Erebus: The Lost Shadow with DejaVu Game Studios, blending my skills in game design and development.

My Vision

Technology is a powerful tool for transformation, and I am committed to creating digital experiences that are intuitive, accessible, and impactful. My goal is to contribute to the development of decentralized solutions that empower individuals and communities, bridging the gap between innovation and real-world needs.
